Verdict: Proceed as a Service Area Business (SAB).

Your face-to-face sessions with organizations in Ahmedabad (e.g., Vastrapur area) and the UK-based client's local office fulfill Google's eligibility criteria. Operating as an SAB allows you to hide your physical coworking address, which is the best practice for protecting your privacy and avoiding any potential "moonlighting" visibility.
