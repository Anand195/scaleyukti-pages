The Problem

63M Indian SMEs need social media.
Most can't afford it.

Social media content is the #1 growth channel for Indian businesses — but it's either too expensive (agencies), too time-consuming (DIY), or too complex (tools). The market is stuck in a broken state.

₹15K

Average monthly agency retainer for social media management in India — inaccessible for most SMEs

6–8 hrs

78%

Indian consumers discover new brands on social media before searching elsewhere — presence is non-negotiable

85%

Businesses now using AI for social media (up from 42% in 2023) — demand for AI tools is proven and exploding

The Triple Pain Point

💸 Too Expensive: ₹15,000–₹50,000/month for an agency is out of reach for 95% of Indian SMEs and solopreneurs.
⏰ Too Slow: Existing AI tools require website logins, prompting knowledge, and manual export to each platform. It still takes 2+ hours/week.
📱 Wrong Channel: Indian SMEs live on WhatsApp — not on SaaS dashboards. Every tool forces them to adopt a new platform. Nobody does.
