Outreach Strategy
Recommended sequence and rules for Ahmedabad CA market

Before You Pitch — Market Intelligence

What Ahmedabad CAs Care About

  • Data stays inside their office — not on any foreign server
  • Their staff can use it without IT training
  • Works with the documents they already receive (scanned PDFs, WhatsApp images)
  • GST filing deadlines are non-negotiable — anything that slows them down is rejected
  • Senior partners decide, but junior staff buy-in matters (they will use it daily)

Tone Rules for This Market

  • Speak in outcomes, not features — "no more GST mismatches" not "vector embeddings"
  • Use ₹ values whenever possible — cost per error, cost per hour, cost per month
  • Reference the ICAI compliance angle — CAs respect regulatory framing
  • Mention "private server in your office" on first contact — kills data fear early
  • Never cold-call on 7th or 20th of any month — GST filing crunch days
