Kaavi/Oxide Flooring: Perfect for Our 550 sq ft New Flat in Ahmedabad

Why Choose This? Traditional Kerala-style Kaavi flooring (lime + red oxide + coconut oil) is 100% natural, zero chemicals—matches your organic prefs. Stays cool in Gujarat heat (5-10°C cooler than tiles), durable 50-100 yrs, affordable ₹80-150/sq ft. Install before furniture since new home![web:23][web:32]

1. Key Benefits (Especially for Hot Ahmedabad Weather)

  • Cooling Effect: Lime's thermal mass absorbs daytime heat, releases at night—no AC needed much in 40°C summers.[web:27][web:47]
  • Health Perks: Barefoot on coconut oil polish is antibacterial, moisturizing; grounding reduces stress/inflammation. VOC-free for clean air.[web:32][web:45]
  • Durable & Eco: Hand-polished shine improves with time; low maintenance, dust-resistant for dry/monsoon.[web:30]
  • Cost Savings: ₹90% cheaper than marble long-term; no replacement every 10 yrs.[web:30][web:46]

2. Can It Go Over Existing Tiles? Waterproof?

Yes, directly on tiles—no removal needed! Roughen tiles (sand/grind), clean, apply base coat, then 3-5 lime/oxide layers + polish. Pros do this in apartments (handles 9th floor).[web:49][web:50] Waterproof? Resistant (sealed for mopping/spills), but not for standing water—vapour permeable prevents trapped moisture/seepage (unlike cement). Wipe spills; great over tiles to avoid subfloor damage.[web:44][web:32]
